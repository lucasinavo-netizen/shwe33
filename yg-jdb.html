Strategy for YG Slots

Bankroll Sizing

For YG high-volatility slots, your session bankroll should cover at least 200-500 spins at your chosen bet level. Example:

  • Bet 500 MMK per spin × 300 spins = 150,000 MMK bankroll minimum
  • Bet 1,000 MMK per spin × 300 spins = 300,000 MMK bankroll minimum
  • Bet 2,000 MMK per spin × 300 spins = 600,000 MMK bankroll minimum

With less bankroll, you're gambling on whether a big win hits before your session funds run out — usually they don't.

Bet Sizing

  1. Flat betting — same bet throughout. Best for YG slots because volatility is already extreme.
  2. Low during dry spells — use minimum bet during long losing streaks to preserve bankroll for when the bonus comes.
  3. Higher on bonus — some players increase bet only during active free spin features if the casino allows mid-round bet changes.
  4. Stop-loss and stop-win — set clear exit points. E.g., "I'll stop if I lose 50,000 MMK or win 200,000 MMK."

Don't chase losses. YG's psychology is brutal — after 50 dry spins you'll feel "a big one is due" and bet higher. This is the gambler's fallacy. Each spin is independent. Big wins are equally likely at any point.
